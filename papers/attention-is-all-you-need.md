# Attention Is All You Need

**Authors:** Vaswani et al.
**Year:** 2017
**Tags:** transformers, attention, nlp

Introduces the Transformer, a sequence-transduction architecture based entirely on attention,
dispensing with recurrence and convolutions. Demonstrates strong machine-translation results
with greater parallelism and reduced training time. A draft pending review.
